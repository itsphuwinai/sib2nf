Fun Facts About Carnations

  • Carnations symbolize love, fascination, and remembrance.
  • They are one of the oldest cultivated flowers, dating back over 2,000 years.

  • Pink carnations are said to have first bloomed from the Virgin Mary's tears.
  • In some cultures, carnations are used in traditional medicine for relaxation and stress relief.
  • Carnations can last up to three weeks after being cut, making them a favorite for bouquets.
  • The scientific name of carnations is Dianthus caryophyllus, meaning "Flower of the Gods."
